About

I have been doing video editing as a hobby since 2019. I fell in love with this art (if you may call it so) soon after I edited my first video. Whenever I see a video that has any sort of intriguing editing in it, I try to decipher its methodology in my head. I follow many youtube channels regarding tutorials and try to keep myself updated on the latest video editing techniques and practices going on in the industry.

My software of choice is Davinci Resolve 17. I am failiar with all the basic video editing techniques, color grading and some VFX in the same. As of now, I am exploring more on the color grading, VFX, and audio editing, and how to implement the same in Resolve 17. My future plans are to learn 3-D VFX in blender as well as in Resolve, and if possible, integrate the two.
